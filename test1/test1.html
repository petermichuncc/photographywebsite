About this theme

Tempo is a Portfolio & Gallery theme with a masonry grid layout. The theme comes in 2 styles: Light and Dark. You can also change the aspect ratio of images easily and there are several choices: Square, Landscape, Portrait or Original aspect. The theme is also compatible with WooCommerce and the Shop page has the same style as the rest of the theme.
